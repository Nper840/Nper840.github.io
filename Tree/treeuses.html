The uses of trees

Trees are usefull for a number of things. Trees are cut down and used for their wood for all sorts of things: construcion, furniture, decorations, etc. Wood used for construction is usually sourced from sustainable patches of fast growing trees, which provides the most efficient and low cost option for companies to make the most profit. Wood is essential in construction, mainly used as the frame for a building because of its strength and low cost. Wood is used as furniture like seats, couches, bookshelves, etc because of its strength and durability. Wood is also preffered more than other materials like metal because of its aesthetic and look.

Trees are also crucial for the worlds ecosystem. They provide homes for billions upon billions of organisms, from bacteriums to great apes. Huge areas of land covered in trees create lush rainforests and jungles that support countless ecosystems and supports amazing biodiversity.
